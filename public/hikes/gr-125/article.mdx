A circular hike of 262km trough a surprising diverse landscape. Passing through numerous picturesque villages and partially along the river Meuse and Sambre. Taste Belgian beer and cheese while hiking near the abbeys of Chimay and Floreffe. The GR125 also goes through Dinant with it’s magnificent Church of Notre Dame de Dinant and brings a visit to the fortified Citadel of Namur.
